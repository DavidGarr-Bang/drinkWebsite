Shop Make Drink

The Beverages was founded in February 2014, when it first started providing cocktails and information now it sells drinks as well as providing information such as recipes. Our simplicity of information and the design makes our website very easy to navigate these wide choices. Our homepage offers you a user-friendly and an up-to-date approach that will lead you to the product range. Our non-stop shopping will allow you to purchase drinks! We have added recipes of our drinks as well as videos (How to videos) We have produce our website as a team for our module called Team Project. In our team we have myself, David, Melba, John, Amit and Rahul.
