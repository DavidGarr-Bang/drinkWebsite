Planters Punch Cocktail Recipe

Posted Mar. 03, 2018

Ingredients

  1. 1 double shot of Gosling's Black Seal dark rum
  2. 2 tablespoons of orange juice
  3. 2 tablespoons of pineapple juice
  4. 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
  5. 2 teaspoons of grenadine
  6. 2 teaspoons of sugar syrup
  7. 3 dashes of Angostura bitters
  8. Cocktail shaker
  9. Ice
  10. Cocktail cherry and pineapple slice to garnish
